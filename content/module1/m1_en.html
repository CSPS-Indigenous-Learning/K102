Introduction

This module provides a brief introduction to Métis people in Canada. You will see that the history of the Métis and their ancestry is complex and varied. Métis identity is still an emerging reality across Canada today, as are the growing legal claims.

Throughout the course, you will be invited to self-reflect on some important questions. We invite you to download this learning journal to capture your thoughts.

Anticipated completion time

90 minutes

Learning objectives

When you have completed this module, you will be able to…

  • identify and use appropriate terminology
  • recognize the diversity of Métis people across the country
  • distinguish historical and contemporary relationships between Métis and the Government of Canada
  • understand contemporary realities of Métis people
